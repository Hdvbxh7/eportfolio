boardgame

Board games are really interesting because of their diversity, but also because of their interactions,

They enable you to meet new people, make new acquaintances or even strengthen ties with friends.

Strategy games will also train reflexion and decision making, some cooperative games will teach you

to work as a team and to organize yourself,memory games will train you to memorize and retain information,

and these are just a few examples that express the diversity and richness of what board games

can bring us psychologically.
