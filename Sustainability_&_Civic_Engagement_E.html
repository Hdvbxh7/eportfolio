AC7PT

I'm in a club at l'n7 called ac7pt, it's a club that aims to enable everyone to understand others and

to put themselves in the place of the other people who inhabit this planet and who may encounter difficulties,

notably due to discrimination, in order to make everyone understand that certain actions shouldn't be taken

because they may hurt people and that just because someone is different doesn't mean we should reject them

or exclude them from a group or worse, insult them.

King KONG TRIAL PLAY

I attended the king Kong diversity-inclusion play in september,this play was interactive and

was displaying the story of a woman being sexually harassed during a night party. And this

story was narrated during a trial where witness including the victims,the friend and the

bully.And all the witness talked one after another showing their side. It was very interesting

as it was displaying a situation that make you reflect on the society and the way things are.It

also help raising awareness about sexism and the way woman are treated some times as

well as inclusion of people outside of groups and bullying. Also it was interesting to see and

hear of the differrents opinion that were voiced because after each testimony and during

each phase of the story, the director of the paly would ask our opinion on the story..I also

found very interesting the way our awareness was raised on how people can interpret some

action and how society see some type of action due to the bias ingrained by society during

childhood.It also helped us learn about harassment and how to react to it. But also how

to help people that are harassed. As for the aspect of the laws i also found that very

interesting since it show how the law works, and how to interpret them

that is always a good things

CLIMATE FRESK

The climate fresk is an activity that is organised during an afternoon where in a group of

eight we are putting card on a big blank piece of paper that show the link between different

aspect of society that affects the environnement and ecosystem and end after placing all of

the cards right by a decoration of the poster and an argument on how we can changed

thinks linked with the difficulty and impact of such measures.I found this activity very

interesting, since it was a way to make us aware of ecology and Also very interactive. It

help us learn about the way things are and what to do to help at our scale. It’s also a good

way to learn of facts in the world that we are not always aware without prior research and

interest on ecology. It also help us learn on how difficult was the situation.But the most

interesting was the last part where we learned about every things that can change the

state of he world.it’s also interesting as it let us hear of the thoughts of other people and

make a time of exchange that let our thoughts evolve and thinks in groups.
